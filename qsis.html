QSIS

Queen's Student Management System

My final year University project was completed within a team of 6 and spanned over 1 year. This was to create a Student Management System for a client, Queen's University of Belfast. I was involved from start to finish of the project; from gathering requirements and database design to testing and presenting the finished system. Most of the project was spent developing, where I enjoyed creating a range of components including a Student Registration Wizard, Timetable creator (with clash detection) and a Notification system.


• Developing using ASP.NET framework, C#, SQL, HTML and CSS.

• Consistent use of frameworks including Bootstrap and FontAwesome.

• Using SVN for version control.

• Using Agile Methodology throughout development process.
